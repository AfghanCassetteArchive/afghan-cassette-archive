Our Mission

The Afghan Cassette Archive is an independent, community-driven effort dedicated to preserving and documenting Afghan music and cassette culture. Cassettes were the primary medium through which Afghan music was distributed, shared, and kept alive — across borders, through diaspora communities, and in living rooms around the world.

The cassettes, audio recordings, images, and related materials presented on this platform are shared strictly for educational, historical, research, and archival purposes. This project operates as a non-commercial, not-for-profit cultural preservation initiative.

All recordings and media featured in the archive remain the property of their respective artists, producers, labels, broadcasters, and copyright holders. We do not claim ownership of any copyrighted material unless explicitly stated. If you are a rights holder and would like your work credited differently or removed, please contact us.
